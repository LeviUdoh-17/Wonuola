About

Oyinwonuola Oluwadamilola Hamza, born on February 18th, 2006, is an alternative artist from Ogun State, Nigeria. She is the firstborn in a family of three and is known for being a bold and outspoken lady.


Oyin's passion for music began at the tender age of 8. She started writing songs and nurtured her passion for music while being a chorister in church. On May 30th, 2024, Oyin released her debut single titled 'Melody' and is hoping to bless ears worldwide with her music.
